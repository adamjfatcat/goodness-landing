The Problem

Nonprofits are forced to run on fragmented fundraising software and manual work.

Donors, prospects, and pledges scattered across spreadsheets and disconnected tools
Events, vendors, and in-kind donations managed with manual processes and email chains
No way to track pledge fulfillment, aging, or at-risk payments
Letters, acknowledgments, and donor communications done one at a time
Reporting requires hours of manual work instead of just asking a question

Goodness connects everything into one intelligent system.

Donors link to pledges. Pledges link to events. Events link to vendors and forms. Communications flow automatically. And when you need a report, you just ask in plain English. Everything is connected because that's how your nonprofit actually runs.

Platform

Nonprofit management software that connects everything.

Six integrated modules that talk to each other — your nonprofit CRM, fundraising, events, and reporting in one place.

Donor & Prospect CRM

Full donor profiles with engagement scoring, activity timelines, tags, and segments. Track prospects separately and convert them as they give. Acknowledge donations with one click.

Pledges & Donations

Manage pledges with payment tracking, fulfillment rates, aging reports, and at-risk alerts. Accept donations via Stripe. See everything linked to donors and events.

Events & Vendors

Run galas, tournaments, and fundraisers with seat maps, attendees, vendor management, in-kind tracking, landing pages, and auto-generated acknowledgment letters.

Forms & Automation

Build branded forms with AI or from scratch. Trigger automations on submission — create tasks, records, send notifications, or call webhooks. Donation, vendor, and prospect forms built in.

AI Reporting

Ask questions in plain English and get instant reports. Donor retention, pledge analytics, year-over-year breakdowns, campaign attribution — plus AI-generated insights and summaries you can export.

Communications

Send emails, SMS via Twilio, and physical letters with merge fields and signatures. Auto-generate PDFs, mailing labels, and in-kind acknowledgment letters. All communications tracked in one hub.

How It Works

Up and running in three simple steps.

2

Donors link to pledges. Events link to vendors and forms. Communications flow automatically. Duplicate records get merged. One unified picture of your nonprofit.

3

Ask, act, grow

Use natural language to generate reports. Get AI insights on donor retention and pledge health. Automate acknowledgments, letters, and follow-ups so your team focuses on mission.
