Pricing FAQ

Common questions about pricing.

The Starter plan includes a full donor CRM, donation forms, basic reporting, email support, and unlimited users — all at no cost. It supports up to 250 contacts, making it ideal for new nonprofits getting started with professional nonprofit management software without any financial commitment.
Absolutely. Our Starter plan is free forever for organizations with up to 250 contacts and includes unlimited users — no per-seat charges. Small nonprofits get access to the same donor CRM, donation forms, and basic reporting as larger organizations. As you grow, you can upgrade to access events, AI automation, and advanced fundraising tools.
No. Every plan includes unlimited users with no per-seat charges. The only additional cost is standard payment processing fees (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction) when you accept donations through Stripe. There are no setup fees, no contract requirements, and no surprise charges.
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. If you switch to annual billing, you save 20% compared to monthly. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period, and you keep access to all features until then.
We'll notify you as you approach your contact limit and suggest the right plan for your organization's size. You won't lose any data or access — we'll work with you to find the best plan. Upgrading is instant and your existing data stays exactly where it is.
